About

The Binghamton University High-Performance Computing Cluster aptly name “Spiedie” is a 143 node cluster housed in the Thomas J. Watson College of Engineering and Applied Science's data center located at the Innovative Technology Complex. This research facility offers compute capabilities for researchers across Binghamton University.
